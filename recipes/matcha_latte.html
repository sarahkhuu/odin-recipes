Iced Matcha Latte Recipe

Description

Matcha is a very popular tea ingredient used in Japan. However it is not just used in tea, but also in desserts and sweet drinks. Matcha also contains caffeine in it, but does not come with the same jitters as coffee. Below is a recipe for an iced matcha latte!

Ingredients

  • ~180-240 mL preferred milk (oat milk recommended)
  • 2g matcha powder
  • 50 mL hot water
  • sweetener of choice (Optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare 50 mL water to 160-175 degrees F.
  2. Sift 2 grams of matcha powder into mixing bowl. Feel free to add extra matcha if desired.
  3. Pour the hot water into mixing bowl. If sweetening with sugar, honey, or other sweetener that requires heat, add that to mixing bowl.
  4. If using bamboo whisk, whisk for 15 to 30 seconds. If using electric hand whisk, whisk for 10 seconds.
  5. Add matcha mixture to milk over ice. If using syrup sweetener, add until latte tastes as desired.
